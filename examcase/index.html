It all started in 1876 when the first phone was invented...

When the phone fever began to spread, people started to connect more and more...

In the telephone central of Aarhus, with the help of the chitchat ladies operating the magic buttons, important stories could be shared...

Today in the very same building, we are trying to connect the past with the present!

We want to continue the tradition of bringing people together and sharing their stories.

So many people shared their stories with us...would you share yours?
